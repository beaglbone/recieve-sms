Receive SMS Online Instantly & Securely

Our platform allows you to receive SMS verification codes online without using your personal phone number. Whether you are signing up for a new website, testing an app, or protecting your privacy, temporary SMS numbers help you stay safe from spam and unwanted messages.

We provide publicly accessible virtual numbers from multiple countries. These numbers can receive OTPs (One-Time Passwords) and verification messages from many popular services. Messages appear instantly on our website, making the process fast and convenient.

Why Use a Temporary SMS Number?

  • Protect your personal phone number from spam.
  • Avoid marketing calls and promotional messages.
  • Quickly verify accounts without SIM registration.
  • Test applications and online services safely.

How It Works

  1. Select a country and choose an available number.
  2. Enter the number on the website where verification is required.
  3. Wait for the SMS to appear on our live message page.
  4. Copy the OTP and complete your registration.

Please note that public numbers are shared. Avoid using them for highly sensitive accounts such as banking or personal financial services.
