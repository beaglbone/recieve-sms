Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

TempNumber does not require account registration. We do not collect personal information such as names, passwords, or personal phone numbers.

However, we may collect basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage statistics to improve our service.

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Maintain and improve website performance
  • Prevent abuse and spam
  • Ensure security and reliability

3. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Temporary Numbers

All temporary phone numbers are public. Messages received on these numbers may be visible to other users. Do not use temporary numbers for sensitive accounts.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools such as analytics providers to monitor performance. These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@tempnumber.com
